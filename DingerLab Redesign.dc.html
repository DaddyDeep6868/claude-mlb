ROUND-ROBIN HEDGE

Mix & match — any two dingers win

Pick up to 5 bats and we build every 2-leg parlay between them — 5 bats makes 10 bets. You don't need a specific pair: any two of your hitters going deep cashes a parlay, and most pairs pay more than the whole card costs.

⚠️
No bet guarantees a green day — this needs two of your bats to homer (if zero or one do, the card loses). But two HRs from a 5-man pool is far likelier than nailing one exact pair — that's the edge. The all-miss downside is shown below.
